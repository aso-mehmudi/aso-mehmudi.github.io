NLP Projects

  • AsoSpell, Central Kurdish Spell-checker, developed based on my master’s thesis, with a business model and online payment in three currencies
  • AsoSoft Library, Open-source library for Kurdish language processing tasks, such as transliterator, normalizer, and sorter
  • VejinBooks, The online Kurdish literary text corpus, collected by a group of 90+ contributors (administrator, developer and editor)
  • VejinLex, The online Kurdish Dictionaries collection (administrator, developer)
  • Kurdinus, JavaScript tools for normalization and transliteration of Kurdish texts (developer)
  • CKMorph, Central Kurdish Morphological Analayzer
  • Kurdish Grapheme-to-Phoneme convertor
  • Kurdish Poem Meter Classifier
  • AsoSoft’s Corpus Tools, A fast, small, and portable Windows application for searching and extracting data in large text corpora, with regex and right-to-left support
